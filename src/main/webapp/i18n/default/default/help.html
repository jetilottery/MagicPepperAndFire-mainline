Hot Pepper Payout

Feel the heat from your spicy wins in Hot Pepper Payout! Reveal red chilies to win, fill a column to unlock multipliers! Enter the inferno bonus where you keep playing until you fill a meter and win the prize, watch out for multipliers to increase your bonus multiplier up to 20x!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the minus button (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the plus button (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Press each Fire Symbol to reveal Chili Symbols.

Reveal a Red Chili Symbol to instantly win the corresponding prize amount. Reveal all Red Chili Symbols in a column to win the corresponding prize multiplier. The corresponding multiplier is applied to each prize in the completed column. Multiple prizes may be awarded.

Reveal a Golden Chili Symbol to trigger the bonus game.

In the bonus game, press each purple tile to reveal different coloured Chili Symbols or multiplier values. Win the prize when the first meter is filled with four of the same coloured Chili Symbols. Only 1 prize may be awarded per bonus.

Starting from 1x, the multiplier meter will increase by adding the value of any revealed multipliers. Revealing a multiplier, will move the multiplier meter along the number of spaces equal to the revealed multiplier value, up to 20x. Once a chili meter is full, the highlighted value on the multiplier meter is applied to the completed chili meter prize for the total bonus win.

The game ends when all symbols and any triggered bonus items are revealed. A message appears, indicating the game's outcome.

Dismiss the win message and view the game's outcome by pressing the message.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game's outcome.

Wins are shown in currency.
